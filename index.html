Why 3D scanning

A precise digital twin of your building.

Instead of fragmented drawings and site photos, you get a single, navigable 3D snapshot of reality—captured once, reused across design, engineering and construction.

Capture once, reuse everywhere

A single scan session provides geometry and imagery that can feed architects, engineers, contractors and facility managers—all from the same source of truth.

Ideal for villas, renovations and complex sites.

Reduce site visits

Explore the space virtually, measure distances, areas and heights after the fact. No more repeated trips just to “double-check” dimensions.

Save time for teams and clients.

Design with confidence

Starting from accurate geometry minimizes clashes, RFIs and change orders. What we build in 3D matches what exists on site.

Better coordination, fewer surprises.

      Process

      From site visit to usable 3D data.

      We combine terrestrial LiDAR, camera rigs and drones to capture the built environment, then align and clean the data in-house before delivery.

      Capture

      01
      Pre-scan planning

      We review drawings (if available), define scope and level of detail, and plan stations and drone flight paths.

      02
      On-site scanning

      We scan interiors and exteriors with LiDAR, and supplement with high-resolution photography and aerial coverage where relevant.

      03
      Quality checks

      Before leaving site, we verify coverage, fill any gaps and confirm critical areas have been captured.

      Processing & delivery

      04
      Registration & cleaning

      Scan data is aligned, denoised and structured in-house. We remove stray geometry and optimize files for performance.

      05
      Data formats

      We can deliver point clouds (E57, RCP, LAS, etc.), meshes or models compatible with your preferred tools.

      06
      Optional modeling

      Where needed, we can convert the scan into a clean 3D model or BIM geometry as a separate scope.
